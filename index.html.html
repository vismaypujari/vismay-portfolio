Featured Case Study
Consumer Behavior & Strategy Consulting · Digital Healthcare

Practo: Consumer Behavior & Growth Strategy Project

Strategies to accelerate GMV growth in India's digital healthcare market — rooted in primary consumer research, behavioral segmentation, and financial modeling.

Core Insight
Practo's GMV growth is constrained more by a trust deficit than by a lack of market awareness.
Business Problem

Digital healthcare adoption is growing at 25%+ CAGR, but consumer trust barriers are limiting conversion and GMV growth. Most users abandon their consultation journey before booking — not due to lack of awareness, but due to trust deficits at a specific inflection point.

Core Finding
Trust deficit > awareness problem
Key Drop-Off
Step-3 doctor comparison = biggest churn moment
Behavioral Pattern
Consumer skepticism drives platform abandonment
Tension Found
Emotional urgency vs. trust conflict blocks conversion
Research Conducted
195 consumer survey responses across 8 Indian cities
23-question instrument across 5 research domains
Secondary market research — WHO, McKinsey, CAGR data
K-means clustering across 5 behavioral variables
Consumer segmentation into 3 distinct clusters
Persona construction — Cautious Priya & Explorer Arjun
Trust barrier analysis and behavioral journey mapping
Competitive landscape mapping — 4-quadrant positioning
Financial impact modeling — 3 scenario projections
Stakeholder interviews — WHO, eSanjeevani, GVK EMRI
Consumer Segmentation — K-Means Clustering

K-means was chosen because it segments respondents simultaneously across 5 numeric variables (trust, convenience, concern about ratings, ease of finding doctors, usage frequency) without imposing predefined categories — suited to exploratory consumer segmentation across a heterogeneous population.

Strategic Recommendations

Four strategies mapped directly to two GMV levers — volume and average transaction value — across the two high-priority consumer segments.

Strategy 01
Emergency Access Mode
One-tap emergency button for verified doctor connection within 5 minutes. Converts highest-urgency moments into premium consultations at ₹500–₹800 per session — targeting Cautious Priya's urgency vs. fear conflict.
+500K sessions/yr · ₹35 Cr revenue
Strategy 02
Symptoms to Solution
AI-guided symptom-to-care pathway that converts passive searches into active bookings. Routes users to mild/moderate/severe care pathways, expanding average order value and cross-selling diagnostics.
+600K consults/yr · ₹48 Cr revenue
Strategy 03
My Personal Doctor
Long-term doctor relationship model driving 3× repeat usage per year. Continuous health records, phygital integration, and health reminders create switching costs and reduce churn from 60% to <20%.
300K users · ₹90 Cr revenue
Strategy 04
Segmented Marketing
Cluster-targeted ad campaigns with distinct messaging for each segment — trust-first for Cluster 1, AI-convenience for Cluster 3, minimal ambient retargeting for Cluster 2. CAC target <₹200.
+8–10% platform lift · ₹30 Cr revenue
